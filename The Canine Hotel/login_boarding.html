The Canine Hotel - Boarding Accommodations

The Canine Hotel is the best choice for Phoenix dog boarding for a reason! All of our suites are custom-built and spacious, many with stylish porcelain tile. Instead of bars or metal cages, private suites with natural light and tempered glass allow your dog to feel more at home while you’re away. During your pet’s stay at our Carson and Long Beach dog boarding facility, all-day play is on the agenda for well-socialized dogs. If your dog needs one-on-one attention, individual play is available.
