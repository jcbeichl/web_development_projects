The Canine Hotel - Grooming/Spa

If your pet could use a new ‘do, why not book an appointment with us? Dog grooming Phoenix pets is super convenient during daycare or boarding. Whether your dog just needs the basics, like a bath and brush-out, or special services, like tooth brushing and ear cleaning, we can help! We can also help your dog get plenty of exercise on our treadmill or in our pool.

We love pampering and dog grooming pets! Indulgent extras such as facials and turndown service (dog boarding guests only) really make your pet feel loved. We’ll work with other Canine Hotel staff to schedule grooming around dog training appointments and all-day play to make sure your dog doesn’t miss a thing while in our care!
