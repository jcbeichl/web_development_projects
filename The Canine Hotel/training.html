The Canine Hotel - Positive Training Program

Do you want your dog to be the best dog on the block? We can help. Our Positive Dog Training program was designed to help you and your dog learn dog-friendly communication and environmental management. Through the use of science-based training principals, we guide you and your pet through fun sessions that allow you both to succeed. We’re sure you both will be feeling like the teacher’s pet after just one class.

We work with puppies and even adult dogs. Give us a call today to learn about our options including one-on-one sessions, group classes, and our ever popular ‘Stay and Train’ program where we work on training basics while your dog is boarding or staying for daycare.
