The Canine Hotel - About

Who better to pamper your pet than professional dog lovers? We are a caring family of pet lovers with diverse backgrounds, experiences, and interests, all brought together because we love dogs!

We are fans of your dogs. We even have certified dog trainers, highly motivated to provide you and your pet a luxury dog boarding experience. We’re groomers and stylists who want nothing more than to give your dog the best grooming salon experience possible. We’re dedicated to creating a safe and fun environment for all during doggie daycare.

At The Canine Hotel, luxury dog boarding is within your reach. Convenient services, such as social doggie daycare, a world-class dog grooming salon, and compassionate training, add value to your pet’s stay. And we believe that every dog deserves the same TLC they get at home with you. We may be a diverse bunch, but we all share something in common with you: We love dogs!

Year-Round Pampering

Give your dog plenty of play and exercise when you drop them off for daycare. Or, let them freshen up with a Pawdicure and Brush-out in our spa.
